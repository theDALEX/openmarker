# OpenMarker

**OpenMarker** is an AI-powered grading assistant designed for teachers and lecturers 📚. It automatically grades essays and reports, making repetitive tasks faster and easier so educators can spend more time **teaching and interacting with students** 🧑‍🎓.

> **Automate grading, save time, and focus on teaching — with OpenMarker.**

---

## Features

- ✅ Automatically grade student essays and reports
- ✅ Download and install **different AI models** to suit your grading needs
- ✅ Make grading **faster, easier, and more consistent**
- ✅ Run locally — perfect for individual educators or small institutions
- ✅ Open-source — freely available to explore, modify, and improve

---

## How It Works

1. Teachers or lecturers install OpenMarker locally on their computer.
2. Students submit assignments (docx).
3. OpenMarker uses AI models to analyze and grade submissions.
4. Educators review suggested grades and feedback, saving time on repetitive work.

> OpenMarker is designed as an **assistant**, not a replacement. Teachers maintain full control over grading decisions.

---

## For Institutions

OpenMarker is designed primarily as a **local, individual-use software** for teachers and lecturers.

While universities or larger institutions could use OpenMarker as a **working prototype**, a full-scale system for centralized grading would require a **different architecture** to handle multiple users, larger datasets, and centralized storage. OpenMarker demonstrates the core functionality and workflow that can be extended for such systems, but it is **not intended for direct large-scale deployment**.
